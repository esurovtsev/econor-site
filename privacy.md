# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Econor s.r.o. handles personal data in connection with this website and the iPhone apps it publishes.

## Controller

Econor s.r.o. is the data controller for the processing described in this policy.

- Company: Econor s.r.o.
- Registered office: Prague, Czech Republic
- Contact: see the [Contact]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) page

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- the website available at `econor.org`
- support and other communications sent to Econor s.r.o.
- iPhone apps published by Econor s.r.o.

## Website Data Practices

The website is a static website hosted through GitHub Pages.

At this time, we do not use our website to collect personal data through forms, accounts, newsletters, analytics, tracking tools, or advertising technologies.

We do not intentionally use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, advertising, or personalization on this website.

As with most websites, hosting providers and network infrastructure may process technical data such as IP addresses, request information, and server logs as part of delivering the website securely and reliably.

## App Data Practices

Our apps are designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.

At this time, Econor apps do not collect personal data from users through their own backend services because we do not operate backend services for them.

Our apps currently do not use:

- user accounts
- analytics
- crash reporting services
- advertising SDKs
- attribution tools
- push notifications
- cloud sync
- subscriptions

Some apps may store non-personal settings locally on the user's device, such as timer preferences or similar app settings. This local storage is used only to make the app function as expected on that device.

## Purchases and Apple

If you purchase or download an app through the Apple App Store, Apple may process personal data related to your Apple account, purchases, payments, and downloads according to Apple's own privacy practices. Econor s.r.o. does not control Apple's processing of that data.

## Support Communications

If you contact us by email, we will process the information you provide in order to respond to your message, provide support, or handle your request.

This may include:

- your email address
- your name, if you include it
- the contents of your message
- any device or app information you choose to share with us for support purposes

The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest in communicating with users and providing support, and, where applicable, taking steps at your request before entering into a contract or performing our contractual obligations.

## Service Providers

We currently rely on third-party providers for limited infrastructure and communications purposes, including:

- GitHub Pages, to host this website
- Zoho Mail, to receive and manage support and contact emails
- Apple, to distribute apps and handle App Store transactions

These providers may process personal data according to their own roles and privacy practices.

## Data Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

For example:

- support emails may be retained for as long as needed to handle the request and maintain a record of support communications
- website-related technical logs, if any are processed by infrastructure providers, are retained according to those providers' own operational practices

## Your Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with similar privacy rights, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection, subject to applicable law.

If you want to exercise such rights, please contact us using the details on the [Contact]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) page.

You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

## Children's Privacy

Our apps and website are intended for a general audience and are not specifically directed to children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the details on the [Contact]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) page.
