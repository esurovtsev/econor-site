# Support

If you need help with an Econor app, please use the contact details on the [Contact]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}) page.

## What to include

When writing to us, please include:

- the app name
- your device model
- your iOS version
- a short description of the issue
- steps to reproduce it, if possible

## What support covers

You can use the same support channel for:

- technical issues
- bug reports
- purchase or access questions
- general feedback
